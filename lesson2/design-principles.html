Contrast

Saver

saver.com

This page fits very well with several concepts of the class, but I chose it in this step to represent the "PARC: Contrast". In the hero image, which has a black background, the main object is a bird, but in contrast there is the name of the company and the button to display the menu. Everything seems one, and it is a pleasant and delicate contrast, but very well achieved.

In this example it is not possible to appreciate it in its entirety, but part of the presentation of this page is closely related to the repetition of objects, in this case smartphones, the same pattern is repeated throughout the page, although it can also be seen in good use of contrast.
